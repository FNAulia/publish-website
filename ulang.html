ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN is a seven-member boy group under Belift Lab. Formed trough the reality show I-LAND, they debuted on November 30, 2020 with their first mini album Border : Day One.

The name of the group was describe as "just like hypens connect different words to make new meanings, the members will connect, discover each other, and grow together".

DIMENSION : DILEMMA

Last November, ENHYPEN comeback with their 1st Studio Album DIMENSION : DILEMMA. The album's three visual concepts SCYLLA, CHARYBDIS, ODDYSEUS each respectively convey the member's upheveal of youth, the ensuing chaos and inner turmoil, and the various new desires they are confronted with in this new world.

ENHYPEN continue to tell their own authentic story, as well as the relatable story of this generation trough a total of eight tracks : "Intro : Whiteout", "Upper side dreamin", "Just A Little Bit", "Go Big or Go Home", "Blockbuster feat. Yeonjun of Tomorrow X Together", "Attention Please!", "Interlude : Question".
